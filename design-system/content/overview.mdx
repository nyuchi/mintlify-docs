## Core principle

Content in the Mukoko ecosystem is **clear, direct, and respectful**. We write for a diverse African audience — people who may speak English as a second or third language, who may be reading on a low-bandwidth connection, and who deserve the same quality of communication as users anywhere else in the world.

These guidelines serve the entire **bundu ecosystem**: mukoko's 17 consumer mini-apps, nyuchi's 7 enterprise products (API Platform, Web Services, Learning, Medical, Rentals, Tools, SEO Manager), and sister brands including Zimbabwe Information Platform and Barstool by Nyuchi. Consistent voice across this family is what makes bundu feel like one ecosystem. *Ndiri nekuti tiri* — I am because we are.
