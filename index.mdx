Welcome to **Nyuchi Docs** — documentation for Nyuchi's portfolio of products and
platforms, built for the reality of African and emerging-market infrastructure.

The **Nyuchi Design System** documented here is Nyuchi's internal design system,
showcased openly — it is one piece of a larger portfolio that also includes the
**Nyuchi API Platform**, **Fundi**, and a growing range of products. **Mzizi** is
the architecture project the design system is built on; **Fundi** is the
self-healing product.
